# Jewelry Image Search Tracking

## Free Image Websites Used
1. Pixabay - https://pixabay.com/images/search/
2. Unsplash - https://unsplash.com/s/photos/
3. Pexels - https://www.pexels.com/search/

## Image Selection Criteria
- **Relevance**: Image must accurately represent the product name
- **Quality**: High resolution, clear details, professional appearance
- **License**: Free for commercial use (CC0, Unsplash License, or similar)
